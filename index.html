Teacher Appreciation


Dear Motiii,
Aap siraf teacher he nhi ho meri bhen bhi ho meri dosy bhi ho.
Jab bhi aapko dekhu apne aap simle aa jati hai or jese aap dusro ko khush rakhti ho vese he khud khush raha karo.
Aap jese teacher har kisi ke naseeb me nhi hote.
Aapko teachers day ki bohat bohat shubhkamnaye.

Shayari for Teacher

Aap kitaabon se zyada dilon ko samajhti ho,
Sawal-jawab se nahi, muskaan se raah banati ho.
Teacher hote hue bhi ek bhen aur dost lagti ho,
College ke safar mein ek hansi ki dhoop ho.
Har mushkil kaam aap aasaan bana deti ho,
Aur apni simplicity se sabka dil jeet leti ho.
